Create New Series

Click on the Plan icon to open the Plan tab. When you open the Plan tab for the very first time, ValQ directs you to lock your model before creating a new plan.

Locking your model ensures the following:

  • The structure of your model, the nodes and their configuration are fixed and wonâ€™t undergo further changes.
    (at least until the planning activities are complete)
  • The reason is that any changes to the underlying model, their relationships, formulae, etc. may make any work-in-progress planning activities inaccurate & irrelevant.
In this regard, ValQ gives you a warning message saying 'Any changes made in the model will re-calculate all the allocations in each series and archive the previous steps'. Therefore, we recommend you to finalize your model before you begin to plan.

The following are the steps to create a new plan on ValQ:

  1. Click on the Lock button once you are ready to plan
  2. Enter the name of the series under Series Name text box
  3. Choose a series to start planning from the drop-down list
    (there are two ways by which you can start here â€“ choose existing series or empty series)
  4. Select the period for which you want to plan from the Periods option
