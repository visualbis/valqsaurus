## Pre-requisite

A ValQ model with simulations performed on it is needed to access Attribution Analysis

## 2. Waterfall chart

<div style={{textAlign: 'justify'}}>
The Waterfall chart can be seen on the left side of the canvas.
The Waterfall chart shows how the simulated KPIs contribute to the change in the outcome KPI.
</div> <br />

<div style={{textAlign: 'justify'}}>
As you hover over the bars, you can see the simulation impact, actual and simulated values.
Click on the full screen option at the bottom to enter the full screen mode.
</div> <br />

<div style={{textAlign: 'center'}}>
  <Zoom>
<img alt="Waterfall Chart Full Screen Mode" src={useBaseUrl('/doc-images/analyze/AAA-4.png')} />
  </Zoom>
</ div> <br />

You can toggle between horizontal and vertical waterfall charts using the options on the top right.

<div style={{textAlign: 'center'}}>
  <Zoom>
<img alt="View Modes" src={useBaseUrl('/doc-images/analyze/AAA-5.png')} />
  </Zoom>
</ div>

## 3. Table

<div style={{textAlign: 'justify'}}>
On the right side of the canvas is the information in the tabular format. 
You can see the baseline and simulated values of the outcome KPI, and the variance in both absolute and 
percentage terms.
</div> <br />

<div style={{textAlign: 'center'}}>
  <Zoom>
<img alt="Attribution Analysis Table View" src={useBaseUrl('/doc-images/analyze/AAA-6.png')}  />
  </Zoom>
</ div> <br />

<div style={{textAlign: 'justify'}}>
Immediately below this, you'll find a list of the KPIs with its baseline and simulated values. 
Simulation impact, Attribution% and Contribution% are also shown.
</div> <br />
