Go to **Analyze > Attribution Analysis** to access this feature.

## Pre-requisite

A ValQ model with simulations performed on it is needed to access Attribution Analysis.

## Features of Attribution Analysis

The Attribution Analysis screen has three sections:

1. [Toolbar](#1-toolbar)
1. [Waterfall chart](#2-waterfall-chart)
1. [Table](#3-table)

## 2. Waterfall chart

The Waterfall chart on the left shows how the simulated nodes contribute to the change in the outcome KPI.

As you hover over the bars, you can see the simulation impact, actual and
  simulated values. Click on the full-screen option at the bottom to enter the
  full-screen mode.

## 3. Table

*Attribution Analysis* details are also listed in a tabular format on the right. 
We have the baseline and simulated values of the outcome KPI, and the variance in both *absolute* and *percentage* terms.

<div style={{ textAlign: "center" }}>
  <Zoom>
    <img
      alt="Attribution Analysis Table View"
      src={useBaseUrl("/doc-images/analyze/aaa-6-attr.png")}
    />
  </Zoom>
</div>

*Attribution analysis tabular format*

The table beneath has a list of the KPIs - its Baseline and Simulated values, Impact, Attribution% and Contribution%

- Impact = Positive/Negative variance on Outcome KPI
- Attribution% = KPI Impact/Outcome KPI Baseline
- Contribution% = KPI Impact/Outcome KPI Variance
